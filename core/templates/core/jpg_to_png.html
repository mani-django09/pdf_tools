About JPG to PNG Conversion

Why convert JPG to PNG? While JPG (or JPEG) is great for photographs, the PNG format offers several advantages for certain types of images. Our free JPG to PNG converter helps you transition from the lossy JPG format to the lossless PNG format, providing better quality for graphics, screenshots, and images with text.

When you convert JPG to PNG with ilovepdf.link, you're upgrading your images from a compressed format to one that preserves all details. Here's why this matters:

Benefits of Converting JPG to PNG

  • Lossless Quality: PNG uses lossless compression, meaning it preserves all image data without degradation—unlike JPG's lossy compression that discards some information.
  • Transparency Support: PNG files support transparency (alpha channel), allowing parts of your image to be see-through—perfect for logos, icons, and overlays.
  • No Compression Artifacts: JPG images often show "blocky" artifacts, especially around text or sharp edges. Converting to PNG eliminates these quality issues.
  • Better for Editing: PNG images are ideal for further editing as they don't degrade with each save, unlike JPGs that lose quality with each edit.
  • Sharper Text and Graphics: Text, lines, and graphics appear sharper and cleaner in PNG format, making it ideal for screenshots, diagrams, and illustrations.
